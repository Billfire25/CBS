Maintenance

Routine maintenance keeps your fireplace safe, efficient, and long lasting, ensuring a warm, cozy home all year.

Clean Thoroughly

We remove ash, soot, and creosote from the fireplace, flue, and chimney. Cleaning prevents hazards and ensures your fireplace works efficiently and safely for many years.

Inspect Components

Our team checks the firebox, vents, and flues carefully. This inspection helps detect damage early and keeps your fireplace operating safely and reliably.

Safety Checks

We perform thorough inspections of dampers, chimney, and flue. Safety checks ensure smooth operation, preventing hazards while keeping your home warm and secure.

Tune Up s

We adjust all parts, clean residues, and fine tune your fireplace. This guarantees proper performance, safety, and consistent warmth for your home.
